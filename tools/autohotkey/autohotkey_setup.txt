Launching Autohotkey .ahk files at Windows startup
==================================================

Run "shell:startup" to take you to your startup folder, e.g.:

C:\Users\jcdc\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup


In here put a shortcut to your .ahk file, e.g. pointing to:

C:\Users\jcdc\Dropbox\scripts\autohotkey\explore.ahk
